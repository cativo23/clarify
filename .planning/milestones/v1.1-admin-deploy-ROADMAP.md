## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Decision: Horizontal bar chart for funnel (Rationale: Easier to read stage names)
- Decision: Gross vs net revenue as solid vs dashed lines (Rationale: Visual distinction without relying solely on color)
- Decision: Package inference from amount ranges (Rationale: Avoids schema changes)
- Decision: Credit adjustments logged as type='adjustment' with amount=0 (Rationale: Audit trail without monetary value)
- Decision: Suspension requires mandatory reason field (Rationale: Compliance)
